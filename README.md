## Project 1: Industrial Spur Gear (Pinterest Reverse-Engineering Study)

### 📸 Visual Model Presentation
![Industrial Spur Gear](gear_screenshot.png)

### 📐 Design Logic & Engineering Takeaways
* **Autonomous Modeling:** Reverse-engineered a mechanical transmission spur gear directly from a raw 2D visual print without video tutorials.
* **Geometric Construction:** Practiced geometric shape blending and precise circular patterning constraints to align gear teeth and mounting slots symmetrically around a central axis.
* **Bearing Integration:** Modeled a concentric counterbore pocket designed to allow standard rotational roller bearings to sit perfectly flush within the hub.
